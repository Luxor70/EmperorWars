Hover Tank

A Hover Tank refers to a type of armored combat vehicle that utilizes hover technology for propulsion and mobility. It is designed for various military operations, ranging from ground assaults to defensive maneuvers.

A Hover Tank is equipped with various types of armament to engage enemy forces. This may include a combination of cannons, missile launchers, machine guns, or energy weapons. The armament is designed to engage enemy infantry, other armored vehicles, or fortified positions.
