Mega Tank

A Mega Tank is typically a formidable war machine, larger and more powerful than conventional tanks. It is designed to provide immense firepower, strong defensive capabilities, and strategic battlefield dominance.

Mega Tanks are equipped with an array of powerful weapons, such as cannons, missiles, and other heavy artillery. They are designed to deliver devastating blows to enemy forces, including other armored vehicles, fortifications, and even enemy mechs.
