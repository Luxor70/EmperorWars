NBC Troops

NBC (Nuclear, Biological, Chemical) Troops refer to specialized military units that are trained and equipped to deploy NBC weapons and conduct offensive operations involving nuclear, biological, or chemical agents. These troops are designed to deliver devastating attacks and exploit the unique capabilities of NBC weapons for strategic or tactical purposes.

These troops are equipped with specialized delivery systems tailored to the type of NBC weapon they employ. This can include missile launchers, artillery platforms, aerial drones, or other vehicles designed to deliver NBC payloads.
