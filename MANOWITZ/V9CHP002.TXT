Brother Battle

The Defenders of the Faith, Protectors of the Faithful. The Brother Battle are Holy Warriors of Celestial Church and one of the elite fighting orders in the Known Worlds. Brother Battle seek out and defend the holy Empire from threats that would seek to destroy its sanctimony. Most Brother Battle (which is open to either gender) train in their military devotions since before the age of ten, sometimes when left on the doorstep of the monastery.
