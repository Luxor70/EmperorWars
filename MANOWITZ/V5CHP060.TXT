House Crusader

The Crusader is a devoted warrior, driven by faith and a sense of duty to a particular religious order or cause. They are often associated with the dominant religion of the Known Worlds, known as the Universal Church, and its various military orders.

Crusaders are deeply devoted to their religious beliefs and serve as champions of their faith. They embody the ideals and principles of their order and often have a strong connection to the religious hierarchy and dogma.
