2nd Republic Frigate

During the Second Republic era, advanced technology and interstellar travel were more prevalent. Frigates were one of the classes of spacecraft utilized by the Second Republic. They typically served as medium-sized warships, possessing a balance of offensive and defensive capabilities.

These frigates were designed to operate in both space combat and patrol roles. They were equipped with various weapon systems, such as energy weapons or missiles, to engage other spacecraft or defend against enemy attacks.
