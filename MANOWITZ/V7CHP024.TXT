Rukh

Mysterious and enigmatic Kurgan World. It was extensively terraformed during the the Second Republic era. Originally the planet was inhabited by enormous reptilian creatures that were hunted to extinction over hundreds of years.

The air is only breathable for a few kilometres above sea level. The equator is notable for is the chemical sea Bahr-i-Khimiya. A thick layer of gases sits on layer of liquids. Fossils of the extinct creatures are visible across the landscape.

It is named after an extinct large flying reptile.
