Armored Bulk Hauler

The Armored Bulk Hauler is a type of spacecraft designed for transporting large quantities of cargo while providing additional protection and defensive capabilities. It is a specialized vessel used for hauling valuable or sensitive goods through potentially dangerous space routes.

It is designed to withstand various threats, such as pirate attacks or hostile encounters, during its journeys. It features reinforced hull plating, advanced shielding systems, and defensive weaponry to deter potential attackers.
