Chem/Bio Warriors

Chem/Bio Warriors are specialized soldiers who are trained and equipped to engage in chemical and biological warfare. They are skilled in handling, deploying, and defending against chemical and biological agents in military operations.

Chem/Bio Warriors are often associated with secretive organizations, such as noble houses, corporations, or clandestine military units. They utilize their knowledge of chemical and biological agents to unleash devastating attacks on their enemies. These warriors are skilled in both the deployment and defense against such weapons, making them a formidable force on the battlefield.
