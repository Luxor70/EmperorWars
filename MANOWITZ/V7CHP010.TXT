Gizeh

Gizeh is a Lost World, a planet that has been cut off from the Jumproutes of the Known Worlds. Lost World with one jumproute to Delphi. Nomads of the Deep Desert, the Durgwhallah survive in a harshly barren realm, and bear a great, abiding respect for the alien Ming. A hospitable people, they are, nonetheless, fierce in their opposition to those who would exploit or harm the Ming.
