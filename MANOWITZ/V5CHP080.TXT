Meson Naval Cruiser

A Meson Naval Cruiser is a large and powerful warship primarily used for planetary defense. It serves as a defensive platform and a deterrent against hostile forces that may attempt to invade or attack.

It is equipped with a long range meson cannon. Meson cannons can have an extended effective range, allowing them to engage targets from a distance. They can be mounted on various platforms, such as starships or ground-based installations, and are often used for long-range engagements or planetary defense.
